How it all started...

The Ford Motor Co. was the first automotive manufacturer in the United States. The company was founded in 1903 by Henry Ford and produced the first automobile on the market, the Model T. Originally the Dodge Brothers worked for the Ford Motor Co. After they gathered some ambition, they decided to branch off on their own and start Dodge Brother Co. They were then sold to Chrysler in 1928, which went into partnership with Fiat and because a multinational company forming Fiat Chrysler Co. General Motors Co. was founded in 1908. All three companies began their competition across the world.
